IIIWEB3HUB Research

The Blockchain &
Web3 Observatory.

Since its founding in 2017, the Observatory has been generating frontier knowledge on blockchain and Web3, shaping the Italian and international market through four foundational pillars.

01 / IV Research

Developing in-depth analyses and studies to understand what blockchain and Web3 are, and what concrete opportunities they generate for businesses and institutions.

02 / IV Network

Building an international community of experts, demand and supply-side actors — facilitating exchange and collaboration to advance responsible adoption of blockchain and Web3.

03 / IV Education

Serving as the reference point for organisations that want to stay updated on market evolutions and technological developments in blockchain and Web3.

04 / IV Communication

Creating culture and spreading knowledge on blockchain and Web3, raising awareness among organisations that could benefit from these technologies.

IVWEB3HUB Projects

Applied research.
Real impact.

WEB3HUB is a pre-competitive, independent research centre that supports organisations in designing, prototyping and certifying blockchain and Web3 solutions — from concept to working prototype.

Why work with WEB3HUB Three fundamental reasons to choose the Politecnico's lab.
i.

Super-partes guarantor.

The Politecnico di Milano's independence ensures objective analysis, free from commercial conflicts of interest — a guarantee that few private partners can match.

ii.

Specialised expertise, years in the field.

Our team brings deep, vertical competence built over years of hands-on work in blockchain and Web3 — combining academic rigour with direct experience on real industry projects.

iii.

Ecosystem development.

Supporting each project means contributing to the growth of the entire blockchain ecosystem — value that extends beyond the individual initiative.

Our projects & services Four lines of work — from analysis to certified prototype.
