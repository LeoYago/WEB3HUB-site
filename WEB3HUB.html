WEB3HUB Research

The Blockchain &
Web3 Observatory

Since its founding in 2017, the Observatory has been generating frontier knowledge on blockchain and Web3, shaping the Italian and international market through four foundational pillars.

Research

Developing in-depth analyses and studies to understand what blockchain and Web3 are, and what concrete opportunities they generate for businesses and institutions.

Network

Building an international community of experts, demand and supply-side actors — facilitating exchange and collaboration to advance responsible adoption of blockchain and Web3.

Education

Serving as the reference point for organisations that want to stay updated on market evolutions and technological developments in blockchain and Web3.

Communication

Creating culture and spreading knowledge on blockchain and Web3, raising awareness among organisations that could benefit from these technologies.

WEB3HUB Projects

Applied Research.
Real Impact.

WEB3HUB is a pre-competitive, independent research centre that supports organisations in designing, prototyping and certifying blockchain and Web3 solutions — from concept to working prototype.

Why Work with WEB3HUB?

Four fundamental reasons to choose the Politecnico's lab.

Super-Partes Guarantor The Politecnico di Milano's independence ensures objective analysis, free from commercial conflicts of interest.
Specialised Expertise, Years in the Field Our team brings deep, vertical competence built over years of hands-on work in blockchain and Web3 — combining academic rigour with direct experience on real industry projects.
Ecosystem Development Supporting each project means contributing to the growth of the entire blockchain ecosystem — value that extends beyond the individual initiative.

Our Projects and Services

Project Design

Analysis & Design

AS IS mapping, functional and technical requirements, TO BE solution design. Politecnico di Milano super-partes certification included.

Prototyping

PoC & Prototype

Selected Project Examples

Real projects delivered in collaboration with leading organisations.

Automated On-Chain Energy Trading

Prototype for a blockchain system enabling automated buy and sell of energy on-chain, exploring the intersection of DLT and energy market infrastructure.

Verifiable Credentials Platform

Solution for issuing and verifying verifiable credentials, designed to enable data interoperability in the procurement domain.

NFT Copyright Protection

Support in developing NFTs associated with multimedia content protected by copyright law, bridging digital creation and intellectual property.

Italian Blockchain Startup Ecosystem

Custom research study on the blockchain startup ecosystem in Italy, co-developed with PwC in 2022.

Rollups & Zero Knowledge Proofs

Dedicated research project on rollup architectures and Zero Knowledge proof technologies for enterprise applications.

Wallet-as-a-Service Selection

Study on Wallet-as-a-Service providers and selection of the optimal blockchain platform for Enel's strategic requirements.

Annual Programme

Events &
Activities

Every year the Observatory runs a structured cycle of events — from intimate closed-door workshops to major national and international conferences — giving partners and sponsors privileged access to research findings and peer networks.

Annual Calendar

March
Kick-Off
Annual Kick-Off Workshop

The Observatory's opening session of the year. Research priorities are presented, the annual agenda is shared, and partners engage in the first structured roundtable of the cycle.

May
Workshop
Research Deep-Dive Workshop

Closed-door session with early-stage research results. Participants include CIOs, CTOs and IT Managers who receive findings directly from Observatory researchers before public release.

June & July
Workshop
Summer Technical Workshops

Focused sessions on specific technical and market themes identified during the first half of the research cycle — with international speakers and live case discussion.

September & October

Autumn Workshops

Knowledge & Insights

Resources
& Publications

Observatory partners and sponsors gain access to an exclusive library of research materials — from in-depth reports to weekly news digests — all produced by WEB3HUB researchers at the Politecnico di Milano.

Research Reports
In-Depth Research Reports

4 to 5 thematic reports published annually, each focusing on a specific blockchain or Web3 research area. Observatory supporters can access all reports free of charge via the Osservatori.net platform.

Access on Osservatori.net
Monthly Newsletter
Blockchain Monthly Overview

A monthly newsletter reserved for the WEB3HUB community, analysing two macro-trends observed during the month and providing comprehensive market monitoring for cryptocurrencies, tokens, DApps, NFTs and DeFi — with rankings, charts and detailed analysis.

Community exclusive
Weekly Digest
Weekly News Digest

A weekly curation of the most important national and international news in blockchain and Web3, delivered directly to the community. Supporters are also able to submit news, content and events for inclusion.
